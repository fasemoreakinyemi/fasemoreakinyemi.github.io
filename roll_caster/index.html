Digital tools to translate research data For Researchers

The mission of the NFDI4Microbiota consortium is to be the central hub in Germany for supporting the microbiology community with access to data, analysis services, data/metadata standards and training.

Learn more

NFDI4Microbiota is a consortium that is part of the German NFDI (National research Data Infrastructure). We recommend this short introduction video to get an overview of the idea behind NFDI
